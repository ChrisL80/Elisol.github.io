Phandalin


General info

Originally a farming community, that was abandoned after several orc raids. Fairly recently it was resettled by farmers and mining prospectors looking for wealth.

There is no functioning government, but a townsmaster is elected to keep records and serve as judge and mediator


Campaign info

Viska and Duon met up with Thrymn and Thoradin in this town and together helped find missing brothers in the nearby mines and rid the town of the group the Redbrands

People/places of interest

Stonehill inn: A modest inn run by a short, friendly young human male named Toblen Stonehill

Barthen's Provisions: The biggest trading post in Phandalin. Run by a human male of fifty years with a kindly manner named Elmar Barthen.

Edermath Orchard: An orchard field with a simple cottage of a silver-haired half-elf named Daran Edermath, a former adventurer

Phandalin Miner's Exchange: A trading post where miners have their finds weighed and paid out. The townsmaster's records were kept here in response of no local lord. Run by an ambitious and calculating human woman named Halia Thornton.

Alderleaf Farm: A simple farm run by a wise female halfling of forty-five named Qelline Alderleaf. She was long-time friends with the druid, Reidoth.

The Sleepyhead inn: The former wateringhole for the Redbrands, now the commandpost of Halia since she took over as townsmaster.

Townmaster's Hall: A small building that worked as the town's council building, when it was run by Harbin Wester. It also houses a small, but serviceable jail.

Tresendar Manor: More of a castle than a manor, it was an ancient building that was abandoned after the orc raids. The cellars had been turned into the hideout of the Redbrands. A nothic lives in the crevace in the cellar
