Gauntlgrymn


General info

Gauntlgrym is an ancient dwarven city and was rediscovered by dwarven king Bruenor Battlehammer after being lost for centuries and occupied by Drow. It is said that the mountain cave in which it is build holds enterances to the underdark.


Campaign info

Heard about the city and king from Eldeth, when the party met her in a Drow prison in the underdark

People/places of interest

Eldeth Feldrun: A female darf scout from Gauntlgrymn, captured by the Drow while scouting in the underdark. Lost sight of her in the escape from the enprisonment. She asked Thoradin to bring word to her family of her fate
