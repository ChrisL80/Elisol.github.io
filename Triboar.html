Triboar


General info

A large town with a population of around 2500. It used to be an assembly point for armies in response to the Orc raids in the area.

The town is ruled by a democratically elected Lord Protector. His or her task is to command the town's militia and to settle disputes.


Campaign info

Not visited by the party, but is mentioned in a letter found on a dead body on the Triboar trail

People/places of interest

Lord Kallish: found a letter to him from a Lord Cornhall of Neverwinter found on the Triboar trail on two dead messengers specifying "We liked the prototype, we would like to order 20. My messengers carry the money"
